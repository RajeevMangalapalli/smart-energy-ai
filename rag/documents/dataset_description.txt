DATASET DESCRIPTION: GERMAN DAILY ELECTRICITY LOAD

Overview:
This dataset provides a historical record of the daily electricity demand (load) in Germany. It is designed for time-series analysis and the development of forecasting models to predict future energy needs.

Data Source:
The data originates from historical records of the German power grid (typically sourced from ENTSO-E or similar transparency platforms).

Key Information:
- Frequency: Daily (aggregated from hourly observations).
- Time Range: January 2015 to approximately September 2020.
- Region: Germany (National level).

Column Definitions:
1. timestamp: The date of the observation in ISO 8601 format.
2. load_mw: The total electricity consumption across the German grid for that day, measured in Megawatts (MW).

This dataset is particularly useful for studying long-term trends, seasonal cycles (weekly and annual), and the impact of holidays or external weather events on national energy consumption.
